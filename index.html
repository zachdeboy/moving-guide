Set yourself up for success from the start

Most moving stress comes from poor planning, not the actual work. Think of this phase as creating your roadmap. By establishing clear systems now, you'll avoid the panic that forces people into expensive last-minute decisions. This week is about understanding the scope of your move and creating the mental framework that will guide all your future decisions.

Understanding the Why:

Key concepts: The moving triangle (time, money, stress - you can optimize two), the decluttering dividend (every item you don't move saves money and effort), and the momentum principle (early wins create psychological energy for harder decisions later).

What's your moving strategy?

Understanding your approach helps prioritize tasks and budget planning. Each strategy has different preparation requirements.
